Crisis Hotlines

When you're in crisis, it's important to know that help is available!
The following crisis hotlines provide immediate support for people in need.

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

Call 1-800-273-TALK (8255) to speak with a trained crisis counselor.

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741 to connect with a trained crisis counselor via text message.

The Trevor Project

Call 1-866-488-7386 or text START to 678678 for LGBTQ+ youth support.
